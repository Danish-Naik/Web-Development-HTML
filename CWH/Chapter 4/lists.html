How to make a cake


Ingredients

    Dry Ingredients

  • 2½ cups (310g) all-purpose flour
  • 2½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1½ cups (300g) granulated sugar

    Wet Ingredients

  • 1 cup (240ml) whole milk (room temperature)
  • ½ cup (120ml) unsalted butter (softened)
  • ½ cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

    Optional for the flavour

  • Zest of 1 lemon or orange
  • ½ cup sour cream or yogurt (makes it extra moist)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease & flour your cake pan.
  2. Mix dry: Flour, baking powder, salt — whisk together.
  3. Cream butter & sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add eggs, one at a time.
  5. Add vanilla and oil.
  6. Alternate adding dry mix and milk — start and end with dry.
  7. Pour into pan, smooth top.
  8. Bake for 30–35 mins. Toothpick should come out clean.
  9. Cool in pan 10 mins, then on rack.
  10. Frost or serve as you like!
